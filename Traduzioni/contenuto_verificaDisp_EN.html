RENT INSTRUMENTATION

To rent an instrumentation, make sure that in "Instrumentation" field the item you're interested in is selected or, eventually, click the arrow on the right and select the one you want. Insert the rent Start Date and End Date in "dd/mm/yyyy" format (put the same date in, if the rent is only one day long); then insert the requested quantity. In the end click on "Check Availability", the page will be refreshed and you will be able to complete the operation.
If chosen instrument it's available in the requested quantity, you can click on "Rent" to complete the operation.
You can check, modify and delete your rentals in "User Panel" section that you can find in the menu.

You can click on "Abort" in every moment to return to previous page.
