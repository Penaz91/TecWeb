OUR ROOMS

RECORDING/REHEARSING ROOM

In this multifunction room most of the audio recording takes place, thanks to acoustic panels designed for a customized room setup.
It can be rented to rehearse alone or with a band, eventually recording the session so you can improve your groove. This can also be helping in the pre-recording phase, in order to quickly find the right feeling with the performer.

AUDIO DIRECTION ROOM

In this room the entire track/album recording session is managed.
From here our technicians decide, togheter with you, the right sound for your musical instruments.

POST PRODUCTION ROOM

In this room we do post-production, mastering and editing works.
Inside this room sounds and melodies come to life, mixed, bent, blended until you gain the maximum results from your musical project. Here you'll see your projects grow up and improve more and more.
